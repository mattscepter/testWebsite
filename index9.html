10. Accessible Tabbed Interface

Accessibility Guidelines

There are several key accessibility guidelines and standards:

  • WCAG 2.1: Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
  • Section 508: US federal regulations for digital accessibility
  • EN 301 549: European accessibility standard
  • ADA: Americans with Disabilities Act

Accessibility features:

  • Proper ARIA role attributes for tabs and panels
  • Keyboard navigation between tabs
  • Visual indication of selected tab
  • Proper state management (selected/unselected)
  • Clear association between tabs and their panels
document.querySelectorAll('[role="tabpanel"]').forEach(panel => { panel.setAttribute('tabindex', '0'); }); });
